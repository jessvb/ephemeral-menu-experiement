Gradual Onset Menu Experiment

Experiment Information

  • This experiment tests the effectiveness of menus with items that gradually fade in based on predicted items (ephemeral adaptation menus) versus regular menus
  • The experiment consists of a few short surveys and blocks of regular menu and ephemeral menu trials
  • You should expect to test the interface for approximately fifteen minutes
  • You will be able to see your progress at the bottom of the screen on the following pages
